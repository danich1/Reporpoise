Druggable Gene Prediction

The Problem


What makes a gene druggable?


A gene is considered druggable if it has a targetable domain.
Possible Examples:
-Neurotransmitter-gated ion-channel transmembrane domain (IPR006029).
-Protein kinase domain (IPR000719).

Problems with definition

  • What protein domains are druggable?
  • What about Biophysical Properties?
    • Gene Mutation Load
    • Transcript Count
    • Molecular Weight
  • Gene Expression?
